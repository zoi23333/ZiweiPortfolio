Introduction

PlantPal is a mobile application created to make plant care easy and engaging for university students. Recognizing that students often lack time and specific knowledge to care for plants, PlantPal provides personalized guidance and reminders, tailoring care instructions to fit the user's schedule and lifestyle. This approach not only simplifies plant maintenance but also promotes a nurturing environment where students can enjoy the benefits of plant care without the usual stress.

Outcomes

+ Conducted research on effective notification systems to engage users.
+ Designed intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces.
+ Developed a functional front-end prototype with responsive features.

User Interfaces

Homepage

The homepage provides a clear overview of plants, tasks, and activities, designed for easy navigation and quick access.

Adding a Plant Process

This flow simplifies adding a new plant, guiding users step-by-step through details and care preferences.

Status

The status page offers real-time updates with clear visuals to communicate plant health and upcoming tasks.

Plant Details Page

The plant details page offers an in-depth view of each individual plant, including its care history, growth progress, and specific care instructions.

Reminder Setting

This section enables users to set personalized plant care reminders, including options for manual input, geolocation, and specifying availability. These features allow users to tailor their plant care schedule according to their preferences and needs.

Front-End Development and Implementation

In the development phase of PlantPair, our team focused on creating the front-end interface of the app.
Our focus was on the front-end development, ensuring the app's design was brought to life with smooth interactions and responsiveness across various devices. While the back-end and data integrations were outside the scope of this project, the front-end was fully developed and ready for future integration.
We utilized Figma, combined with Locofy (the AI tool), to translate our UI designs into a functional front-end layout, ensuring a seamless user experience.
Figma: For creating and iterating on the UI/UX design.
Locofy.ai: These were the primary technologies used for implementing the front-end, allowing us to build a responsive and interactive user interface.
TypeScript & React Native: These were the primary technologies used for implementing the front-end, allowing us to build a responsive and interactive user interface.
The result was a well-crafted front-end that provided users with an intuitive and visually appealing interface.
