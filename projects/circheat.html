Crafting Sustainability: The Journey of Redesigning Heating Systems for Circularity.

Introduction

In our collaboration with Viessmann, we embarked on a transformative project, CircHeat, aiming to reimagine the end-of-life process for 11.5 million domestic boilers by 2045. Our objective was to establish a more sustainable, circular approach to decommissioning these systems, enhancing resource efficiency and aligning with global sustainability targets.

Outcomes

+Designed a circular take-back system for Viessmann’s decommissioned heating systems, enhancing resource recovery and reusability.
+Developed a logging app prototype to facilitate the tracking of heating system components and support circularity in operations.
+Conducted a business viability analysis showing the potential for cost efficiency through the reuse, refurbishing, and recycling of heating system parts.

Project Vision

Our vision with CircHeat was clear:
+ Develop a take-back system that incorporates circular economy principles.
+ Ensure the system is user-friendly and aligns with the needs of all stakeholders including technicians, end-users, and manufacturers.
+ Utilize technology to simplify the decommissioning process, making it more efficient and less wasteful.
